## Why DevPel

Gaza and the West Bank are full of
engineers shipping real things —
APIs, apps, tools for their own
communities. Most of that work was
scattered across private repos and
group chats, invisible to anyone
outside them.

## What changed

We built one place to post it,
credit everyone who built it, and
find the next person to build with.
